AlertEDU

AlertEDU is a notification mobile application dedicated to instantly and efficiently connecting students and faculty of a university community. A user can create a group and send notifications to all students, staff, and/or professors during severe weather or dangerous situations; student organizations can also send event reminders. Additionally, it allows for hassle-free communication without using cumbersome text messages or contact lists, and can all be organized into one place. Communication within the alert system is limited to those who are within a university’s network.
