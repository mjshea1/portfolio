Will Leather Goods

Dossier: Email Strategy & Design

Will Leather Goods - 2014 to 2015

Despite the multitude of advancements in new social medias and digital marketing technologies, email remains a leading marketing tool and is the strongest means for successfully engaging the audience. Establishing and maintaining a focused email strategy, especially for an online retailer, is an imperative addition to a company’s marketing plan. As the project lead for Will Leather Good’s email marketing strategy, I am responsible for the design, implementation, and dissemination of the marketing emails. Will Leather Goods operates on a bi-weekly email plan to promote new materials, upcoming events, holidays, promotions, and even a regular Spotify list.

Purpose & Objectives

Will Leather Goods sought to expand and develop a more in-depth digital marketing strategy to increase product awareness, and ultimately revenue, within the increasingly competitive online retail marketplace. Moreover, the campaign is designed to increase social media interaction, and keep consumers informed on product releases, company news, promotions, and events.

Samples from a month long coordinated Valentine's marketing campaign.

Results & Effectiveness

The results of the newly implemented email marketing campaign has been very significant. The amount of emails sent weekly has increased regularly to two per week, which has resulted in increased web traffic and online sales. Additionally, the number of new patrons signing up for emails increase by 600%. Open rates increased by 12%, and click through rates also experienced a remarkable increase.
