Safe Zone

I designed the UA Safe Zone booklet, sticker, and window cling to help inform and educate faculty, staff, and students of The University of Alabama about LGBTQA sensitivity, and promote its awareness on campus. The window clings and stickers serve as beacons to inform the campus community of the locations of Safe Zone Allies. The booklet is divided into six sections that contain information, definitions and a “how to” guide for dealing with issues that might arise on campus. I created all the materials from concept to completion in order to bring my design to life.

Branding, Print - The University of Alabama Safe Zone - November 2012
