Spirit of Tuscaloosa

The Spirit of Tuscaloosa logo was created for a candlelight vigil held in honor and remembrance of those affected by the 2011 tornadoes that destroyed central Alabama, and killed 43 people. Thousands attended the event held in Tuscaloosa on June 1, 2011. The logo I created was used on promotional and PSA materials including handouts, fliers, and social media. Read the Article
