Get On Board Day

Dossier: Event Branding

UA: Center for Instructional Technology - January 2012

What the Tech?! is a multifaceted brand identity campaign and event that included marketing and branding materials such as brochures, QR codes, posters, an interactive information booth, smartphone materials, and social media that would be premiered at the University of Alabama’s Get On Board Day - an information expo that provides campus organizations and departments the opportunity to promote their services to the campus community.

Purpose & Objectives

The the new Center for Instructional Technology requested a marketing campaign for Get On Board Day. The objective of this campaign was to promote awareness about the new Center for Instructional Technology (CIT), and inform faculty, staff, and students about the services that CIT can provide. Additionally, CIT wanted to increase its social media presence and get the campus community actively engaged. The interactive photo / information booth encouraged people to have their photos taken, and later viewed on the CIT social media sites to increase likes and shares by online participation. The booth was designed to be a full service location and individuals could have instant gratification by using iPads located at the booth to login to social media sites and see pictures, or they could participate later at home.

Outcomes & Effectiveness

The campaign resulted in over 400 table surveys being completed, and increased Facebook like total by 300%. Many photos were taken and uploaded to Facebook, and numerous people set them as their profile pictures, and tagged, shared, and liked them, further inceasing CIT’s brand awareness. Additionally, the CIT booth was recognized as the most interactive and effective booth at Get On Board Day that year.
