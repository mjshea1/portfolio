About Me

From a young age I knew design was a field I wanted to eventually pursue as a career. Art and design is not only my profession, it is my hobby, my passion, my life. In order to grow my skills and talent within this ever-changing field, I developed a fascination with what technology can achieve—the computer is the medium I use to express my design ability. Continuing along an artistic journey, I often find myself innovating new and progressive approaches to design that are reflected in the creativity and concepts of the projects featured on this site.
